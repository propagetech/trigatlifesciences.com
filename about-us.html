OUR COMPANY

About Us

We are a young company founded by Pharma professionals having vast marketing experience in the field of pharmaceuticals, registered in the year 2007 under the indian companies act 1956. We have a pool of personnel with high managerial and technical competence and also the skilled and educated workforce in the marketing. Our company has intended to develop a good marketing network of class by investing a lot to establish and strengthen the area in providing info through technology. We are determined to tap the market economy of pharmaceuticals and to grab the rightful share in the pharma market which is currently growing at the rate of 20% or above.

OUR COMPANY is intended to make available medicinal products at cost effective prices to suit the underprivileged patients who are unable to get treated for the disease.
Our vision is to become a leading player in the Indian market by providing quality and affordable medicines for the treatment of life threatening diseases. We look forward to making rapid strides in filling up therapy gaps by introducing innovative products in times to come. Considering today's scenario of healthcare, the company will look forward to enhancing its focus on high growth niche area like-Cardiovascular, Anti-diabetics and CNS products.

Manufacturing Unit
